About Us

Our vision

Our vision is to bring the love of Pokemon to people's doorsteps. We plan to expand the store and get shipments from Japan. We value Pokemon fans of all ages and hope to create a community that welcomes all. This group is meant for causal video gamers to trading card players, even just plush collectors.
