Our Story

NicMic Games is a video game development studio that was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate gamers who shared a vision of creating innovative, engaging, and memorable gaming experiences. The studio is headquartered in Stevens Point, Wisconsin and has since grown into a major player in the gaming industry.

NicMic Games has become renowned for its commitment to innovation and creativity, often pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the gaming industry. The studio's team of developers, designers, and artists are passionate about their work and are dedicated to delivering exceptional gaming experiences that stand out in a crowded and competitive marketplace.
